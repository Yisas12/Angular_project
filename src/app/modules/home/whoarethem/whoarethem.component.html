Who are Rick and Morty?

Rick and Morty are two guys (a grandfather and a grandchild) that live a lot of adventures.
Rick is the most intelligent grandfather in the world. He creates a lot of machines like a teleportation gun or a spacecraft to go to another planets. Morty is not the best grandchild but they love themselves so much.
They are always living an adventure in other planet or in other galaxy despite being scolded by their relatives. They are:

  • Summer Smith: Morty's sister
  • Beth Smith: Morty's mother and Rick's daughter
  • Jerry Smith: Morty's father
